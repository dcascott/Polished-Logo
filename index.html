Logo Variations

I wanted something that expressed me and that can be used in a variety of different situations. Simple, fun, friendly, professional, creative, and versitile. I purely focused on my initals, my name which I felt were a odd combination, and my name. My name entire name has a few round character as well. I looked in to cursive and signitures and type face that have curly letters and model my logo from that. I recreated parts of the charaters and connected them. I wanted my letters to be connected as representing making connections.

In most situations I would use the rainbow gradient logo. In more more formal situation I could use the black logo. For darker screens, I would use the inverted or rainbow gradient logo with white text. I added my logo in other colors to show it versitility. I could maybe use these colors in certain printing situations but more than likely, I will rarely change the color of the logo.
